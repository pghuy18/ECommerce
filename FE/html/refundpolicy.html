Vietjet Air

  • If you purchase an Eco or Skyboss ticket, you are only permitted to modify the name on your ticket. Additionally, the Promo ticket class will have the ability to modify the name.
  • If you wish to modify the date of your Vietjet Air flight, you must do it at least 3 hours before departure.
  • You must pay costs such as the Vietjet flight date change charge, the Vietjet flight time change fee, and the ticket price difference when changing Vietjet flights.
  • For a period of 180 days, ticket reservations are only valid for Skyboss ticket classes.
